About Us

A World Class Classified Company

We take pride in leading regional tourism innovation and development.

Even in these challenging times with a decrease in the number of foreign tourists visiting Korea, we are dedicated to showcasing the beauty and charm of our region to the world.

Our primary goal is to develop special travel services tailored for foreign tourists and U.S. military personnel.

Through this, we aim to provide foreign tourists with a sense of wonder by introducing them to Korea's diverse cultures and experiences while simultaneously contributing to the local economy.

In addition, we are actively collaborating with local residents and the elderly population to revitalize regional tourism and establish a strategy of mutual prosperity.

We seek to foster community engagement and promote regional development by harnessing local experiences to develop diverse content and by establishing share houses through remodeling for the elderly population.

We are dedicated to sustainable tourism and the development of the local community, and we look forward to working together toward a better future. Thank you for joining us on this journey.

Why Us

In these challenging times, we are determined to develop unique travel services for foreign tourists visiting Korea and the U.S. military personnel, once again showcasing Korea's beauty and charm to the world. Through our collaboration with USO KOREA, we offer a wealth of programs and experiences.

We actively collaborate with local residents and the elderly population to revitalize regional tourism and pursue a strategy of mutual prosperity. In pursuit of this goal, we are dedicated to the following efforts.

  • Development of Local Experience Content
  • Formation of Share Houses through Remodeling for the Elderly
  • Offering Unique Travel Experiences for Diverse Foreign Tourists and U.S. Military Personnel
  • Local Development through Community Engagement

Exploring Cultures Through Experiences

We are pioneering new horizons in local tourism, fostering cultural exploration to encounter diverse worlds. In a time when the number of foreign tourists visiting Korea has declined, we aim to be a place where various cultures are experienced and shared.

  • Our core values are cultural exchange and understanding.
  • We strengthen collaboration with the U.S. military in Korea to promote and offer an increased number of tourism programs.
  • We aim to engage with the local community through projects involving the elderly population.
